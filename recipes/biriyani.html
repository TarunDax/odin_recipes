Biriyani

Description:

Biryani is a layered rice dish that's popular in South Asia and is made with meat, spices, and rice

Biryani's taste is a delightful mix of aromatic spices, tender meat, and flavorful rice, creating a rich and savory experience

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Mutton meat
  • Garam masala
  • Onions
  • Tomatos
  • Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Mint leaves
  • Water
  • Ghee

Steps:

  1. Wash the mutton
  2. Marinate the mutton
  3. Boil the rice and heat ghee in a pan
  4. Fry the onions
  5. Cook the marinated meat
  6. Layer the rice & mutton
  7. Let the biryani cook for 10-12 minutes
  8. Garnish and serve
