Dragon Chicken

Description:

Dragon chicken is a delicious Indo-Chinese appetizer.

Crispy chicken strips and bell peppers are tossed in a spicy sauce with crunchy cashews.

Ingredients:

  • Boneless Chicken breast pieces
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Sambal belek
  • oil
  • Light soy voice
  • Rice vinegar
  • Red & Green bell peppers
  • Green onions
  • Cashew nuts
  • Egg white
  • Kashmiri chili
  • ketchup
  • black pepper
  • Dried chilli
  • Flour
  • Corn flour
  • salt
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Fry the chicken pieces
  3. Make Sauce
  4. Toss fried chicken with the sauce
  5. Garnish with green onions
